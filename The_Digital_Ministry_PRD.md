# **1\. Executive Summary**

The Digital Ministry is a comprehensive platform designed to equip churches and ministry leaders with professional design resources and tools for digital ministry. The platform aims to provide high-quality social media graphics, stock photos, sermon series graphics, screen slides, and scheduling tools to help churches maximize their digital presence and ministry impact.

# **2\. Product Overview**

## **2.1 Vision**

To empower churches and ministry leaders with better design tools and resources, enabling them to focus more time on ministry rather than design work.

## **2.2 Mission**

Deliver professional-grade design resources and digital tools that enhance church communication, streamline content creation, and amplify ministry reach in the digital space.

## **2.3 Target Audience**

* Church pastors and ministry leaders  
* Church communication teams and media coordinators  
* Small to medium-sized churches with limited design resources  
* Youth ministry leaders and community groups  
* Independent ministry organizations and nonprofits

# **3\. Core Features & Requirements**

## **3.1 Social Media Graphics**

### **Description**

Pre-designed, customizable graphics optimized for various social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube.

### **Key Requirements**

* Multiple format options (square, landscape, portrait, story)  
* Categorized by themes (seasonal, sermon series, events, announcements)  
* Editable text layers and color schemes  
* High-resolution exports (PNG, JPG)  
* Template library with weekly/monthly additions

## **3.2 Stock Photos Library**

### **Description**

Curated collection of high-quality, ministry-relevant stock photography.

### **Key Requirements**

* Search and filter functionality (by category, color, orientation)  
* Categories: worship, community, prayer, youth, family, leadership  
* License-free images for commercial ministry use  
* Multiple resolution downloads  
* Collections and favorites functionality

## **3.3 SZN Kits (Seasonal Kits)**

### **Description**

Comprehensive design packages for specific seasons or church calendar events (Christmas, Easter, Summer, Back to School, etc.).

### **Key Requirements**

* Complete branding packages with consistent visual identity  
* Includes social graphics, slides, print materials  
* Customizable color palettes and fonts  
* Download as complete package or individual assets

## **3.4 Shorts Creation Tool**

### **Description**

Tool for creating short-form video content optimized for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok.

### **Key Requirements**

* Vertical video format (9:16 aspect ratio)  
* Text overlay and caption tools  
* Music library integration  
* Template library for quick content creation  
* Export options for different platforms

## **3.5 Sermon Series Graphics**

### **Description**

Complete graphic packages for sermon series including titles, promotional materials, and social media content.

### **Key Requirements**

* Cohesive design across all materials  
* Title slide templates  
* Social media announcement graphics  
* Bulletin and print materials  
* Multi-week series support

## **3.6 Screen Slides (Presentation Templates)**

### **Description**

Ready-to-use presentation slides for worship services, announcements, and teaching sessions.

### **Key Requirements**

* Compatible with PowerPoint, Keynote, and ProPresenter  
* Multiple design styles (modern, minimal, bold, elegant)  
* Lyric slides, scripture slides, announcement slides  
* Easy text customization  
* Background options (image, video, solid color)

## **3.7 Social Media Scheduler**

### **Description**

Content planning and scheduling tool for managing church social media presence across multiple platforms.

### **Key Requirements**

* Multi-platform support (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X)  
* Calendar view for content planning  
* Post preview functionality  
* Draft and approval workflow  
* Analytics and engagement tracking  
* Team collaboration features

# **4\. Technical Requirements**

## **4.1 Platform & Infrastructure**

* Web-based platform accessible via modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)  
* Responsive design for mobile and tablet access  
* Cloud-based storage for assets and user content  
* CDN integration for fast asset delivery

## **4.2 Performance Requirements**

* Page load time: \< 3 seconds  
* Support for 10,000+ concurrent users  
* 99.9% uptime SLA  
* Automated backups every 24 hours

## **4.3 Security Requirements**

* SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission  
* OAuth 2.0 authentication integration  
* Role-based access control (RBAC)  
* GDPR and data privacy compliance  
* Regular security audits and penetration testing

## **4.4 Integration Requirements**

* Social media API integrations (Meta, Twitter/X, YouTube)  
* Payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal)  
* Email service integration (SendGrid, Mailchimp)  
* Analytics integration (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)

# **5\. User Experience Requirements**

## **5.1 User Interface**

* Clean, modern design aesthetic similar to Canva and Notion  
* Intuitive navigation with clear information architecture  
* Drag-and-drop functionality for asset management  
* Quick preview capabilities for all assets  
* Dark mode option

## **5.2 Onboarding**

* Simple registration process (email or social sign-in)  
* Interactive product tour for new users  
* Sample templates and resources immediately available  
* Welcome email series with tips and tutorials

## **5.3 Accessibility**

* WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance  
* Keyboard navigation support  
* Screen reader compatibility  
* Color contrast ratios meeting accessibility standards

# **6\. Business Requirements**

## **6.1 Subscription Model**

| Tier | Features |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Free** | Limited access to basic templates and stock photos (up to 10 downloads/month) |
| **Basic** | Full access to templates, stock photos, and screen slides. Limited shorts creation (up to 5/month) |
| **Pro** | Everything in Basic \+ unlimited shorts, social media scheduler, SZN kits, priority support |
| **Team** | Everything in Pro \+ team collaboration tools, custom branding, dedicated account manager, up to 10 team members |

## **6.2 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

* User Acquisition: 5,000 registered users in first 6 months  
* Conversion Rate: 15% free to paid conversion  
* User Retention: 70% month-over-month retention  
* Monthly Active Users (MAU): 60% of registered users  
* Customer Satisfaction: NPS score of 50+  
* Average Downloads per User: 25+ assets per month

# **8\. Development Roadmap**

## **Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-3)**

* Stock photos library (1,000+ images)

* Free and Basic subscription tiers

## **Phase 2: Core Features (Months 4-6)**

## **Phase 3: Advanced Tools (Months 7-9)**

* Shorts creation tool  
* Social media scheduler  
* Team collaboration features  
* Analytics dashboard  
* Team subscription tier launch

## **Phase 4: Scale & Optimize (Months 10-12)**

* Mobile app development (iOS and Android)  
* API for third-party integrations  
* Custom branding tools  
* Advanced analytics and reporting  
* Community features and user marketplace

# **9\. Risks & Mitigation Strategies**

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Low user adoption | High \- affects revenue and growth | Strong marketing campaign, partnership with church networks, free trial period |
| Copyright/licensing issues | High \- legal and financial risk | Clear licensing agreements, original content creation, legal review of all assets |
| Competition from established platforms | Medium \- market saturation | Focus on niche (ministry-specific), superior user experience, competitive pricing |
| Technical scalability issues | Medium \- user experience degradation | Cloud infrastructure with auto-scaling, load testing, performance monitoring |
| Content quality concerns | High \- affects brand reputation | Professional design team, quality control process, user feedback integration |
