Frequently
Asked Questions

Is this event beginner friendly?

+

Yes! While we have advanced tracks for veterans, "Day 01" includes a dedicated foundation track designed to get you up to speed with the latest generative tools and concepts.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

+

For Workshop sessions, a laptop is required. We recommend a machine capable of running WebGL smoothly (M1/M2 Mac or equivalent PC with discrete GPU).

Are meals included in the ticket?

+

Standard and VIP tickets include breakfast, lunch, and unlimited coffee. Virtual tickets obviously do not include digital food (yet).
