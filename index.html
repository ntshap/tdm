Core Values

  • Christ-centered

    Acknowledges God's sovereignty, the authority of the Bible, Lordship of Jesus, and power of the Holy Spirit. All for God's glory.

  • Cutting Edge

    Committed to continue learning. Not just reacting but leading and innovating.

  • Digital Integrity

    Pursuing holiness in the real world means pursuing a holy life in cyberspace.

  • Gospel-driven

    Driven by the Bible. Not fame, trends, or personal ambitions.

  • Spirit-led

    Led by the will of the Spirit, a heart ready to be led, taught, and formed.

Can I use these for my church stream?

+

Yes! All assets come with a commercial license for ministry use, including live streams, social media, and printed bulletins.

No. We provide editable templates that work right in your browser. However, we also provide PSD files for advanced users.
